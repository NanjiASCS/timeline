Destiny Timeline

Destiny Year 1 2014-2015

Destiny launches in September 2014 and has two DLC's throughout the year. These were "The Dark Below" and "The House of Wolves" During this time "The Vault of Glass" and "Crota's End" raids and the "Prison of Elders" arena were the prominent endgame activities.

In September 2015 "The Taken King" DLC was released, adding more content to the game. This is commonly regarded as the best DLC the hame has had. "King's Fall" was the featured raid and is known as the hardest raid in the games history. A revamped version of Prison of Elders known as "Challenge of Elders" was added in April.

Destiny Year 3 "Rise of Iron" 2016-2017

September 2016 kicked off with the "Rise of Iron" DLC. This was my personal favorite year for this game. This DLC featured "Wrath of the Machine" my favorite raid of all time. In April the "Age of Triumph" update was added that brought all the old raids to current level reviving the gear from them back into the game.

Destiny 2 Year 1 "Factory Reset" 2017-2018

In September 2017 "Destiny 2" launched easily starting the worst year in the games history. They had launched the "Curse of Osiris" and "Warmind" DLCs. The featured raid was "Leviathan" and two mini rades in the same location known as "Eater of Worlds" and "Spire of Stars."

Destiny 2 Year 2 "Forsaken" 2018-2019 (Current)

September 2018 revived the game as the "Forsaken" DLC had launched. The player base had revived and the game was in a good state. The featured content was Forsaken's "Last Wish" raid as well as two smaller raids added in the Annual Pass known as "Scourge of the Past" and "Crown of Sorrow." This is where we are now.
